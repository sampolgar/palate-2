Opportunity Mapping

Opportunity 1 - Automate wine purchase to cellaring

Outcome: Users automatically update their wine collection from purchasing to drinking

Opportunity: Purchase to Cellar Tracker Automation

Solutions:

  • Website widget to automate purchase to cellar
  • Email address to send receipts to - AI to monitor and upload to your cellar
  • Screenshot reader
  • Integration with known wine ERP

Experiments

  • Prototype the widget for technical feasibility - will the widget read the web page purchases
  • Prototype Cellar Tracker integration - is it capable
  • Qualitative interviews - is it worth paying for?

Opportunity 2 - Find wine selling opportunities

Outcome: find/sell current wine

Opportunity: don’t know how to sell my wine

Solutions:

  • List cellar tracker inventory on a public marketplace
  • Cellar Tracker integration with Liv Ex selling
  • App to sell wine

Experiments

  • Qualitative interviews with Cellar Tracker user base to assess selling requirements
  • Feasibility prototype for Cellar Tracker & Liv Ex integration

Opportunity 3 - Can’t easily track en-primeur wine purchases

Outcome:

  • users can track en-primeur wine purchases

Opportunity:

  • Buyers can’t easily track en-primeur purchases

Solutions:

  • Wine shop/distributor to send you a receipt of the purchase
  • Wineshop login
  • Wine shop/distributor to send you a digital token of the purchase
  • Winery creates receipt which can be delivered from winery to distributor to customer
  • Login to a webpage to view your purchases

Experiments

Wine shop/distributor to send you a digital token of the purchase

  • 10 x user research with websites in AUS that sell en-primeur
  • 10 x user research with buyers who buy en-primeur wine
  • Figma prototype for widget
